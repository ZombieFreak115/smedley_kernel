Detailed Description

CCountry corresponds a playable country in Victoria 2. It also includes entities not commonly though of as countries, such as the Rebel Country (owner of the rebel units and placeholder for rebel controlled territory), as well as the NULL country (owner of the "unowned" uncolonized territory in-game).
